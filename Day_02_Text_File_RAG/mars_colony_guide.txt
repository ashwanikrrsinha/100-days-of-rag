Welcome to the brave new world of Mars Colony Alpha.
Established in the year 2045 by Elon's cyborg clone, "Musk-X".
The currency on Mars is called "Red-Credits".
One Red-Credit is equal to 50 Earth Dollars.
To breathe outside, you must wear the "Aero-2000" suit.
The most popular sport is "Crater-Hopping", played on Tuesdays.
Warning: Do not feed the Martian Sand-Cats. They eat titanium.
